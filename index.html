Our Services

At Majorpromet, we take pride in offering a wide range of printing and finishing services tailored to meet your needs. From offset printing on large formats to precise screen printing on various materials, our advanced equipment ensures top-notch results. We also provide thermal lamination for added durability and a professional touch to your projects. Whether you're working on something small or large, we bring expertise and quality to every job.

Offset Printing

We are equipped with a state-of-the-art 4-color printing machine, capable of delivering vibrant, high-quality prints on paper sizes up to B1.

Screen Printing

We are also equipped with an advanced automatic screen printing machine, enabling us to print on sheet materials up to B1 size with precision and efficiency. No matter the product, we can deliver high-quality prints.

Film Lamination

We also have a fully automatic thermal lamination machine, capable of laminating paper up to 100x100 cm with flawless precision. This allows us to add a professional, durable finish to any project, enhancing both its appearance and longevity.
